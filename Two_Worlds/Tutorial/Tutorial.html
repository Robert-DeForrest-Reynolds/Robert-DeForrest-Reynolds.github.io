Two Realms

In

the year 25643, most civilizations had been driven near the brink of destruction as a result of the thousands of years spent warring with one another over the control of resources. With the realization that the wars were themselves were depleting the very resources they were fighting over, citizens of the planets Titan and Analis began working together to create a simulation. This simulation was ment to be battlefield where the best militants from both planets could battle it out as many times as needed until there was a clear victor. This would also allow them to determine the outcome of future conflicts outside of their reality, saving both their people from dying.

When Analis was ultimately tasked with powering the simulation, it was understood that to do so, would require an enormous amount of energy. So much so, that nearly all the scientists of Analis reached the decision to build a Dyson Sphere around a local black hole to siphon its energy, and so they did. However, the simulation became unstable and essentially imploded, then exploded which caused the two planets to become surrounded and stuffed into a part of reality. The phenomena was inexplicable, even the greatest scientists of Analis had no clue where they were, or what to think of it. A purple void space, surrounded by nothing, seemingly empty forever.

Later, it became theorized by Titans, that the two were always meant to war and are now stuck in a time-loop in which they will war forever. Unless, a victor is somehow deemed by the simulation, which many believe is impossible. However, if a vicitor is established, the Titans believ the simulation will close and it will no longer siphone energy from the black hole. This is the most well accepted theory as of now on Titan, but Analis refuses this, and even mocks it.

Titan,

the planet of brute culture and nature, where living through hardship is inevitable, and independance is a sign of a great warrior. As young kids, Titan's are forced out into the natural world to learn to fend for themselves and to train for the many battles they are to face. Once they've become masters of their environment, they are then forced into service for the military in some form, whether as a warrior or healer. The civilian populus of Titan, should not be underestimated, as most of them are ex-military or have been involved with military in some manner at some point in their life.

Analis,

the planet of knowledge, and comprehension, where success is believed to be attained through codification, and structure. To the Anali, war is won through the use of complex strategies and great intelligence. The children of this planet begin mandatory schooling at 8 months of age, and are embued with the idea that knowledge is the ultimate weapon. Analis has birthed and forged some of the most elite agents in the universe.

How to Play

When

you join the discord server, you will be presented with the option to choose a team. Each team represents a planet that you can choose to help with their fight. Once you choose your planet, you are then locked to specific channels, and cannot use the opposing planets channels.

You can then do the following to help your planet:
  • Buy
  • Sell
  • Salvage
  • Mine
  • Farm
  • Construct
  • Craft
  • Market
  • Send
The simulation between the planets is ongoing and for both planets, %70 of the population is civilian, and the other %30 is military. You can use the send command to decide what they what to send for the upcoming skirmish. A skirmish will take place every 2-4 hours. When a planet has %12 of the opposing planets population or less, they lose, and the skirmish is over.

Each planet has unique traits, abilities, weapons, shields, and much more to take advantage of, and players have the opportunity to switch teams if they'd like every simulation. You can choose what type of playstyle you'd like to use. You can be a weapons manufacturer or a shield manufacturer. Decide whether to contribute to the munitions or to build the defenses. It's a team game in the end!
