Two Realms

Titan is a planet of brute culture, used to hardship, living with nature, and doing things on one's own. As young kids, they are forced out into the natural world to fend for themselves, training to become great warriors. Once they've attained mastery of their environment, it is mandatory to serve some form of duty to the military, whether as a warrior or healer. The civilian populus is a capable one, even if not military, as most are ex-military or have been involved in some manner at some point in their life.

Analis is a planet of knowledge, and understanding. They believe success is attained through codification, and structure. They believe war is won abusing exploiting strategies with great intelligence. Children begin mandatory schooling as 8 months of age, and are embued with the idea that knowledge is the ultimate power. Some of the most elite agents within within the universe were birthed, and forged Analis.

In the year 25643, driven near the brink of destruction, depleted of almost all resources, the surviving citizens of Titan and Analis worked in unison to create a simulation where the best militants from both world could battle it out as many times as needed until there is a clear victor. This would allow them to decide conflicts outside of their reality, saving both their people from dying. Analis ultimately was tasked with starting the simulation, and to run it took enormous amount of power. Such that the scientists of Analis built a Dyson Sphere around a local black hole to siphon enough power from it. However the simulation essentially imploded, then exploded, surrounding and stuffing the two planets into part of reality even Analis scientist had no clue where they were, or what to think of it. A purple void space, surrounded by nothing, seemingly empty forever.

Later it is theorized by Titan scientists that Titan and Analis were always meant to war, and they are now stuck in a time-loop of sorts, in which they will war forever, until somehow a victor is deemed by the simulation, and the simulation closes, which stops it siphoning power from the black hole. This is the most well accepted theory as of now on Titan, but Analis refuses this, and mocks it.

How to Play

When you join the discord server, you will be presented with the option to choose a team. Each team represents a planet that you can choose either to join or to fight. Once you choose your planet, you are then locked to specific channels, and cannot use the opposing planets channels.

You can then do the following to help your planet:

  • Buy
  • Sell
  • Salvage
  • Mine
  • Farm
  • Construct
  • Craft
  • Market
  • Send

The simulation between the planets goes on for 2 weeks. For both planets, %70 of the civilization is civilian, and the other %30 is military. Each day a skirmish will take place every 2-4 hours. Players can use the send command to decide what they what to send for the next skirmish. You cannot queue sending anything for skirmishes, anything sent by a player will be used in the next skirmish. When a planet has %12 of the opposing planets population or less, they lose, and the simulation resets.

Each planet has unique traits, abilities, weapons, shields, and much more to take advantage of, and players have the opportunity to switch teams if they'd like every simulation.

Players can choose what type of playstyle they'd like to use. You can be a weapons manufacturer, shield manufacturer. You can build hospitals, or make food. You can build schools, or and teach the populus to better technology. It's a team game in the end!
